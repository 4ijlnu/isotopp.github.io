<p>Tumblr of the Day are two Podcasts in German language. Ecomento reported on Volkswagen ([article in German](https://ecomento.tv/2017/07/12/vw-markenvorstand-wir-koennen-tesla-stoppen/)):</p>
<p>> Volkswagen CMO: We can stop Tesla […] CMO Herbert Diess reinforces his statement that he does not think that Tesla has lead to which Volkswagen can't catch up. "We do see Volkswagen as a company that can Tesla, because we have capabilities and skills Tesla currently hasn't."</p>
<p> Posting this lead to a discussion, which contained also the usual misconceptions of electric cars. So here are two useful Podcasts in German in e-Mobility:<!--more--><br />
- Clean Electric ([iTunes](https://itunes.apple.com/de/podcast/cleanelectric-podcast/id1073923579?mt=2), [Website](http://www.cleanelectric.de/)) has a lot of episodes with back-listening potential. The episodes [Mehr als man denkt](http://www.cleanelectric.de/episode-3-mehr-als-man-denkt/)&nbsp;and&nbsp;[Mehr als man denkt II](http://www.cleanelectric.de/mehr-als-man-denkt-2/) are discussing the various models available on the market. The Episode [Wer? Wie? Was? Fragestunde mit Cleanelectric](http://www.cleanelectric.de/fragestunde/) discusses the realities of e-Mobility from the POV of a guest. Mileage, charging systems, charging providers and stuff are all covered.<br />
- Electrify BW ([iTunes](https://itunes.apple.com/de/podcast/electrify-bw/id1185215600?mt=2), [Website](http://electrify-bw.de/), [Podcast category](http://electrify-bw.de/category/podcast/) on that website) is newer, but similarly covers e-Mobility from a swabian perspective. [Alles rund ums Laden](http://electrify-bw.de/electrify-bw-der-podcast-2-alles-rund-ums-laden/) also discusses mileage, and also plug systems and similar stuff, and Oma muß ins Krankenhaus covers [electricity providers](http://electrify-bw.de/electrify-bw-der-podcast-3-die-oma-muss-ins-krankenhaus/). [Elektrisch transportieren](http://electrify-bw.de/electrify-bw-der-podcast-4-elektrisch-transportieren/) covers electric Transporters.</p>
